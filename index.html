Financial Math

Net Present Value (NPV)

$$ \mathrm{NPV}=\sum_{t=1}^{T}\frac{CF_t}{(1+r)^t} $$

CAPM Expected Return

$$ E[R_i] = R_f + \beta_i \big( E[R_m] - R_f \big) $$

Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR)

$$ \mathrm{CAGR}=\left(\frac{V_f}{V_0}\right)^{1/n}-1 $$
